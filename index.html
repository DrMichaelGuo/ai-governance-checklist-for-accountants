Step-by-step instructions

1

Navigate the checklist

Click on each section header to expand and view the detailed checklist items. Each section covers a different aspect of AI governance.

2

Update item status

Use the dropdown menu next to each item to select the appropriate status (Pending, In Progress, Complete, or N/A) based on your current implementation.

3

Add evidence and links

In the evidence field, document your implementation evidence, file references, or paste links to relevant documents. These details help with audit trails and compliance verification.

4

Monitor progress

View your overall progress in the Progress Overview section, which displays completion rates for each governance area and an overall completion percentage.

5

Export your data

Click the "Export Data" button in the navigation bar to download your checklist data. Choose between CSV format (for Excel or spreadsheet programmes) or JSON format (for developers or data import).

Tip: All changes are automatically saved in your browser. For permanent storage, export your data regularly to keep a backup of your progress.
